Learning Outcomes

This project was built to explore and understand quantum computing fundamentals through practical implementation. Key concepts learned include:

  • Quantum Superposition: States existing in multiple configurations simultaneously
  • Quantum Entanglement: Non-local correlations between quantum systems
  • Quantum Measurement: Wave function collapse and probability interpretation
  • Quantum Gates: Unitary operations manipulating quantum states
  • Quantum Parallelism: Performing multiple computations simultaneously
  • Quantum Advantage: When quantum algorithms outperform classical counterparts
