Lessons in building products: small bets, fast feedback

October 27, 2025 • 5 min read

This short essay covers product lessons I've learned while shipping small projects: ship small, get feedback, iterate.

Key Principles

In product development, three principles stand out:

  1. Ship a minimal, testable slice quickly.
  2. Talk to real users early and measure what's working.
  3. Iterate on what matters: retention, speed, and clarity.

I try to blend product thinking with practical engineering to deliver features that actually help people.
